About

Welcome to my homepage! My name is Xutao Mao. Currently, I am fortunated to be a research assistant in Hong Kong Baptist University advised by Prof. Bo Han.

Prior to this, I obtained a BSc degree in Computer Sceince and Mathematics from Vanderbilt University. I was fortunated to be supervised by Prof. Tyler Derr and Prof. Dan Lin there.

My current research focuses on investigating the principle to build trustworthy AI especially focus on improving the alignment and safety in multimodal language model reasoning.
